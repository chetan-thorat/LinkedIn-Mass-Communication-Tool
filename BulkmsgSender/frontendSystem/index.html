Selecting and Unselecting Followers Instrunction:

To select a follower: Click on the name of the follower in the list. The selected follower will appear in the "Selected Followers" section below. To unselect a follower: Click on the name of the selected follower in the "Selected Followers" section. The follower will be removed from the selected list.
